    Our Goal

    Help Seniors Remember and Thrive The Brain Exercise Initiative uses simple math,
    writing and reading aloud exercises as an
    intervention to improve cognition function in
    those with Alzheimer's Disease

    Volunteer

    Passionate about neurodegenerative diseases and/or Alzheimer's? Become a part of our community of volunteers
    to engage with seniors in retirement homes in
    the Seattle area by leading read aloud
    passages, math exercises, and simple writing
    tasks!
